Hello

My name is Alex Pinkus

I was a kid with an interest in just about everything, but especially math and music. My third love, gaming, often conflicted with diving deeper in any one area of academia.
However, I managed to graduate with a B.S. in Mathematics and a Master's in Audio Sciences with the intention of becoming a recording engineer. After graduating though, I realized that if I became a game sound designer, I could finally find a symbiotic relationship between my three passions.
Plus, if I went back in time to tell myself at age 10 that he's going to be a sound designer for video games, I think he would be pretty cool with that. Although he may be a little miffed that I did not go into bioengineering to invent real-life dragons.

I currently work from home in IT and freelance tutor at night (mostly math and ACT/SAT prep). I produce music under the name Riley Circles and play piano, guitar, and percussion. At Peabody Institute, I primarily did recording projects involving new compositions in classical, jazz, and rock. Other topics I studied include acoustics, audio technology, digital signal processing, psychoacoustics, and composition.

~These are a few of my favorite things~
